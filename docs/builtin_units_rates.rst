Units of rate
=============

Arc-seconds per year
--------------------


.. code-block:: php

    new Rate(new ArcSecond(float $angle), new Year(1));
    Rate::makeUnit(float $number, Rate::EPSG_ARC_SECONDS_PER_YEAR)
    Rate::makeUnit(float $number, 'urn:ogc:def:uom:EPSG::1043')
